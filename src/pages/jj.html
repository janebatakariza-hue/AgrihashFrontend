The process
How MediVex works
STEP 01

Submit a claim

Type or paste any health statement you've seen online or heard from someone around you.

STEP 02

Keyword analysis

MediVex scans your text against a curated database built from WHO, CDC, NIH and UNICEF guidelines.

STEP 03

Clear verdict

Get an instant plain-language result — Verified, Misleading, or False — with an explanation and sources.

Understanding results
What each verdict means

Verified

This claim is directly supported by trusted global medical organisations. You can confidently rely on this information.

Misleading

The claim has partial truth but is missing important context, uses exaggerated language, or distorts the actual medical picture.

False

This claim directly contradicts established medical evidence and could cause serious harm if acted upon without professional guidance.
